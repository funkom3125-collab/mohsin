About Me

My name is Mohsin. I’m 17 years old, originally from Pakistan and raised in England, United Kingdom. I’m an active and driven person with a strong interest in self-improvement and personal growth.

In my free time, I enjoy going to the gym and staying fit, as well as playing football and cricket. I’m always open to trying new things, learning new skills, and pushing myself to improve both physically and mentally.

Education

I have attended The Royal School, Wolverhampton since Year 7. I am currently in Sixth Form (Year 12), where I am studying A-Levels in BTEC IT, Economics, and Maths.

I particularly enjoy Maths because it develops problem-solving skills and logical thinking that can open many opportunities for my future.

Gym

At the gym, I follow a workout split to keep my training balanced across different muscle groups. I also make sure to take rest days when needed so my body can recover properly and I don’t feel drained the next day.

Alongside training, I try to maintain a good diet with plenty of protein to support recovery and help me feel my best.
